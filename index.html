Assumptions & Methodology

  • Employee TCO includes salary, burden, attrition, and management/QA overhead.
  • AI cost includes platform + usage + human-in-the-loop + one-time implementation.
  • Residual labor is the non-automated workload adjusted by productivity lift and rework.
  • Scenario range adjusts automation, rework, and AI usage to reflect uncertainty.
  • Year-1 ROI is calculated as annual net savings divided by total Year-1 AI program cost.
  • Confidence score increases when payback is fast and downside scenario remains positive.

AI vs Traditional Team Model

  • Traditional model scales linearly with headcount and overhead.
  • AI model front-loads implementation, then scales with volume.
  • Best outcomes come from AI + human review on high-risk tasks.

FAQ

Is AI more expensive than humans?

It depends on volume, quality requirements, and implementation cost. At higher repeatable volumes, AI often reduces cost per unit.

How much does it actually cost to use AI?

Typical cost includes platform fees, usage fees, monitoring, and human oversight. This calculator separates each line item.

How much does AI cost per minute?

Per-minute cost depends on token/model usage, workflow complexity, and review rates. Convert monthly usage into unit economics for your team volume.

What is the average cost of AI?

There is no single average. Costs vary by task type, model selection, and the level of human quality control you require.
