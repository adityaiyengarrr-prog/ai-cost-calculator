AI vs Traditional

FAQ

How do I calculate AI token costs?

Multiply your input tokens and output tokens by each model rate, then multiply by call volume. This calculator automates that formula.

How many words is 1,000 tokens?

A common planning assumption is around 750 words for 1,000 tokens in English, though this varies by language and formatting.

Is this an exact invoice prediction?

No. It is a directional estimate and should be validated against provider billing.

Why are some models marked for verification?

Some providers/models did not have a clear public token price at implementation time. Those entries are intentionally flagged instead of guessed.
